About

Hanan Gharib Elzeiny is a Senior HR Expert with over 30 years of experience across diverse business sectors. She brings together deep technical HR knowledge, academic credentials to doctorate level, and a distinguished record as a trainer and facilitator to deliver measurable organizational change.

Her practice centers on Strategic Planning Facilitation — having guided more than 20 companies through structured strategic planning processes — alongside performance management systems design, competency framework development, and HR policy architecture. She is a certified Trainer of Trainers, having built internal training capacity within organizations and delivered more than 30 rounds of the HR Management Diploma in both Arabic and English.

Hanan is a certified Eunoia® Personal Construct Assessor (Sweden), reflecting her commitment to evidence-based people assessment and culture change. Her expertise spans both private sector organizations and operational environments including Lean Manufacturing settings.
