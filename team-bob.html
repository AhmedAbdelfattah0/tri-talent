About Us

Ehab Gawad is an accomplished professional in Learning, Leadership, and Coaching with over 25 years of experience, including 13 years in the Saudi market. His expertise spans various industries such as retail, tech, logistics, and food. Ehab has a strong background in executive and business coaching, leadership development, facilitation, and training, as well as talent management and development.

He has successfully driven change management initiatives, enhanced employee engagement, and shaped organizational culture and values. Ehab holds various certifications, including CIPD in HR, Associate Certified Coach (ACC) from the International Coach Federation (ICF), Certified Executive Coach, and more.

His extensive experience and certifications make him a reliable change catalyst, capable of driving organizational transformation and leadership development.
