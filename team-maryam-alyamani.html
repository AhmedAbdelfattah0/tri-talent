About

Maryam Alyamani is a Marketing Communications expert with over 16 years of experience spanning journalism, press, communications, public relations, and digital marketing. Based in Jeddah, KSA, she brings together editorial depth, brand strategy, and data-driven digital execution into an integrated communications practice.

She is the Founder of The M-HUB Agency, a full-service marketing and communications firm, and a published author of the children's novel Princess Liliana. Her client portfolio spans FMCG, automotive, hospitality, healthcare, and government sectors.

As Head of Communications at Mohammed Yousuf Naghi Motors — the Ford and Lincoln distributor in Saudi Arabia — she delivered transformational results, growing digital lead contributions to retail sales from 7–12% to 40%, achieving 2,370% growth in after-sales lead generation, and being recognized as a leading distributor in the MENA region for campaign management and digital excellence.
