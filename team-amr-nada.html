About

Dr. Amr Nada is an International Master Trainer, executive educator, and commercial leader with more than thirty years of professional experience across FMCG, retail, distribution, and marketing services. He combines multinational executive leadership experience at Procter & Gamble, Pepsi Al Jomaih, and major retail groups with deep academic and executive training practice.

He designs and delivers executive and management development programs in sales excellence, leadership, negotiation, communication, and governance. He teaches across undergraduate, graduate, MBA, and executive education platforms, and has trained professionals and leadership teams across more than one hundred organizations in the Middle East and Central Eastern Europe.

His work integrates evidence-based training frameworks with real operating business experience, making him a trusted partner for organizations seeking measurable capability transformation.
